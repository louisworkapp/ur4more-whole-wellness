## 1) One-Sentence Pitch

**UR4MORE in one sentence:**  
A holistic wellness app that delivers evidence-based physical, mental, and spiritual health tools with optional faith integration, guiding users toward Jesus Christ as their True North while respecting their choice at every step.

**Who it's for:**  
Adults (13+) seeking wellness, personal growth, and meaningful changeâ€”from those wanting secular tools to committed believers wanting integrated spiritual wellness.

**Primary pain it solves:**  
Fragmented wellness tools that ignore the whole person; secular apps that miss deeper meaning; faith apps that lack clinical rigor; lack of consent-respecting faith integration.

## 2) Brand DNA

**Mission:**  
*(To be defined - inferred from creed and philosophy)*  
Guide users from darkness into light through evidence-based wellness tools and respectful faith integration, pointing toward Jesus Christ as ultimate truth and healing.

**Vision:**  
*(To be defined)*

**Core Values:**

- **Truth Axis**: Every secular concept maps to biblical truth

- **Clinical Excellence**: Evidence-based (CBT/MI/ACT), licensed oversight
- **Theological Accuracy**: KJV-only, doctrinal consistency, interdenominational respect

**"Non-negotiables" (what we will NOT do):**
- Coerce users into faith content
- Compromise clinical standards for any reason
- Use prohibited content (yoga, chakra, mantra, tarot, astrology, namaste)
- Store sensitive mental health data without consent
- Provide diagnosis or treatment (coaching/self-help only)
- Force faith overlays without consent (Light mode requires per-session consent)

## 3) Target Users + TAM Strategy

**Top 3 User Personas:**

1. **"The Seeker" (Secular-First)**
   - Age: 25-45
   - Situation: Wellness-focused, open to growth, spiritual but not religious
   - Biggest struggle: Finding meaning in wellness journey, fragmented tools
   - Desired outcome: Holistic wellness with optional deeper purpose
   - TAM: Largest top-of-funnel

2. **"The Faith-Curious" (Light Mode)**
   - Age: 30-50
   - Situation: Active in wellness, exploring faith, wants gentle integration
   - Biggest struggle: Finding authentic faith that enhances (not dominates) wellness
   - Desired outcome: Balanced integration of faith and wellness
   - TAM: Secondary, high-conversion potential

3. **"The Disciple" (Kingdom Builder)**
   - Age: 25-60
   - Situation: Committed believer, wants comprehensive spiritual wellness integration
   - Biggest struggle: Finding quality faith-integrated wellness tools with clinical rigor
   - Desired outcome: Deep discipleship with evidence-based wellness
   - TAM: Smaller but highest LTV

**Which persona is the "largest TAM" top-of-funnel:**  
"The Seeker" (Secular-First) - largest addressable market, designed to convert to Light mode over time

**Secondary personas:**
- Faith-curious: Exploring gentle faith integration
- Committed believers: Wanting comprehensive spiritual wellness
- Church leaders: Seeking tools for congregations
- Recovery community: Faith-integrated sobriety support

**Regions/languages planned:**  
- Primary: English (US/UK/CA/AU)
- Future: Spanish, Portuguese, other languages (not yet implemented)

## 4) Core Product: Daily Coach

**The "daily loop" (what happens each day):**

**Morning:**
- Morning Check-In (optional): Mood, energy, urge, faith levels; focus areas (Mind/Body/Spirit)
- Daily Wisdom/Inspiration card (faith-aware quotes)
- Planner: AI-suggested activities, calendar view, commit to plan
- Alarm/notification: Configurable morning alarm (default 7 AM)

**Midday:**
- Quick access to Mind Coach for reframing, breathing exercises
- Body workouts available
- Course/lesson access
- Nudges based on plan commitments

**Evening:**
- Daily Check-In (5-step flow):
  1. RPE Scale (required) - physical exertion level
  2. Pain Assessment (optional)
  3. Urge Tracking (optional) - cravings/triggers
  4. Coping Strategies (optional) - AI-suggested based on urge/pain
  5. Journal Entry (optional) - free-form reflection
- Completion summary with points earned
- Streak tracking (7-day milestones)
- PM check-in reminder (default 8 PM, configurable)

**Habit system (streaks, goals, milestones, rewards):**
- **Streaks**: 7-day milestone awards, current streak display
- **Points System**: 
  - Daily check-in completion: Base points
  - Coping strategies completed: +25 points
  - Journal entry (120+ words): +10 points
  - Workouts: +25 points
  - Mind sessions: +20 points
  - Spiritual devotions: +30 points
  - Course completion: +35 points per week
- **Milestones**: Weekly goals (body workouts, mind sessions, devotions)
- **Rewards**: Points redeemable in marketplace (physical products, digital content, experiences, donations)

**Modules included:**
- **Breathing**: 4 presets (Quick Calm 2min, HRV 6min, Focus 3min, Sleep 6min)
- **Mind Wellness**: Mind Coach (CBT/MI/ACT), reframing exercises, urge management
- **Body Fitness**: Workouts, pain relief guides, mobility exercises
- **Spiritual Growth**: Daily devotions, scripture, prayer, creed
- **Courses**: 12-week discipleship, 7-day Light onramp, Mind Aim Up 8-week
- **Planner**: Daily planning, calendar view, AI suggestions, commitment tracking
- **Rewards Marketplace**: Points redemption for products/content
- **Journaling**: Integrated in daily check-in, free-form entries

**Faith modes (Faith-Off â†’ Faith-Neutral â†’ Kingdom Builder):**

**Faith Mode Definitions:**
- **OFF (Secular-First)**: No spiritual content; fully useful secular tools; conversion invites at natural progression points
- **Light (Gentle Integration)**: Minimal faith content; per-session consent for verse/prayer overlays; optional devotions
- **Disciple (Active Integration)**: Daily devotions enabled; faith overlays available; discipleship courses accessible
- **Kingdom Builder (Complete Integration)**: Full spiritual integration; mission focus; leadership tools; all content accessible

**What changes in UI copy, content, and coach behavior for each mode:**

*OFF Mode:*
- UI: "Choose what leads to life" (secular framing)
- Content: No scripture, no faith-based coping suggestions, no spiritual rewards
- Coach: "Aligned to truth" (secular reframe messages)

*Light Mode:*
- UI: "Walk in the Light" with optional verse reveal
- Content: Consent-required verse/prayer overlays, optional devotions, some faith rewards
- Coach: "Aligned to Christ's truth â€” [verse ref]" with consent-button for verse reveal

*Disciple/Kingdom Builder:*
- UI: Full creed display, scripture carousel, "Present your body a living sacrifice (Rom 12:1)"
- Content: All faith content available, auto-suggested faith-based coping for high urges, full rewards access
- Coach: Faith-enhanced messages, identity-in-Christ overlays, prayer suggestions

**What triggers mode suggestions (and consent rules):**
- **Week completion**: After completing Mind Coach week, show "Meaning Horizon" card (dual CTA: upgrade or keep secular)
- **High-urge resolved**: "Want a peace that keeps watch over your mind?" invite after resolving urge 7+
- **Values milestone**: When user selects service/legacy/sacrifice values in values clarification
- **Rate limiting**: Max 1 invite per day, 7-day snooze on "Not now", weekly counter resets Sundays
- **Consent rules**: Light mode requires per-session consent for overlays (resets next session); always provide "Keep Secular Tools" option

**Key coach behaviors:**

**Tone/voice:**
- Supportive, evidence-based, non-judgmental
- OFF: Clinical, professional, secular wisdom
- Activated: Same clinical foundation + gentle faith encouragement
- Quotes: Viktor Frankl, Marcus Aurelius (OFF); KJV scripture, UR4MORE originals (Activated)

**Boundaries (medical/mental health disclaimers, sensitive topics):**
- Self-help/coaching only; no diagnosis or treatment
- Crisis referral protocols: Emergency resources, crisis hotlines
- Minimum age: 13+ with parental consent for minors
- Professional disclaimers on all therapeutic content
- Licensed therapist oversight via Clinical Review Board

**Personalization inputs (what the user tells us):**
- Faith mode preference
- Mood, energy, urge levels (daily)
- Pain levels, coping strategies used
- Workout preferences, equipment available
- Course progress, lesson completion
- Plan commitments, focus areas
- Journal entries (stored locally with consent)

**Memory rules (what we store, what we never store):**
- **Store**: Faith mode, progress, points, streaks, course completion, preferences, plan commitments
- **Never store**: Sensitive mental health details, journal content (local-only option), crisis conversations
- **Privacy levels**: Standard (full tracking), Strict (aggregate only), Opt-out (minimal)

- **Every PR**: Both checklists must pass before merge

**AI + content interaction rules (what AI can generate vs must be curated):**
- **AI can generate**: Personalized suggestions, reframe responses, workout recommendations, plan suggestions
- **Must be curated**: Scripture (KJV-only, human-reviewed), devotional content, course lessons, quotes (human-written UR4MORE Originals)
- **Guardrails**: AI suggestions filtered through brand rules, faith mode gating, content safety checks

## 9) Technical Architecture (what's already in the repo)

**Repo overview (monorepo? apps/packages?):**
- Single Flutter app repository
- Gateway service (Python/FastAPI) in `gateway/` directory
- Shared assets in `assets/` directory
- Tools/scripts in `tools/` directory

**Frontend stack:**
- **Framework**: Flutter 3.35.6 (Dart 3.9.2)
- **UI**: Material Design 3, custom theme (Midnight Minimal, Frame.io-inspired)
- **State**: Provider pattern, SettingsScope (inherited widgets)
- **Storage**: SharedPreferences (local), planned: backend sync
- **Responsive**: Sizer package, 430px max-width clamp, text scaler 1.0
- **Navigation**: Named routes (AppRoutes), bottom navigation bar

**Backend stack:**
- **Gateway**: Python 3.x, FastAPI, uvicorn
- **API**: RESTful endpoints (/content/quotes, /content/scripture, /content/devotionals, /health, /content/manifest)
- **Auth**: JWT-based (HS256), kid-based key rotation
- **External providers**: Pluggable system (quotes, scripture, devotionals) with allowlist
- **Caching**: In-memory caching for content

**Database:**
- **Current**: SharedPreferences (local only)
- **Planned**: Backend database (PostgreSQL/MongoDB TBD) for:
  - User profiles, progress, points, streaks
  - Check-in history, journal entries (with consent)
  - Course progress, plan commitments
  - Marketplace transactions
  - Analytics events

**Auth:**
- **Current**: AuthService (local), phone/SMS authentication (SMS autofill)
- **Backend**: JWT tokens (kid-based rotation)
- **Planned**: OAuth (Google, Apple), email/password, church member linking

**Payments:**
- **Current**: Points-only (no real payments)
- **Planned**: Stripe integration for subscriptions, product purchases
- **Revenue sharing**: 20% subscriptions, 30% products to churches (planned)

**AI stack (model, RAG, vector DB, prompting, guardrails):**
- **Current**: Rule-based AI suggestions (AI Suggestion Service)
- **Planned**: LLM integration (model TBD) for:
  - Personalized reframe suggestions
  - Activity recommendations
  - Coping strategy matching
- **Guardrails**: Brand rules filtering, faith mode gating, content safety checks
- **RAG/Vector DB**: Not yet implemented (planned for personalized content retrieval)

**Analytics:**
- **Current**: Telemetry service (debug prints, structured events)
- **Events tracked**: app_opened, checkin_completed, faith_mode_changed, workout_done, feature_used, streak_7_award, redeem, etc.
- **Planned**: Firebase Analytics, Mixpanel, or Amplitude integration
- **Privacy levels**: Standard (full), Strict (aggregate), Opt-out (minimal)

**Hosting/deployments:**
- **Frontend**: Flutter builds (iOS, Android, Web)
- **Gateway**: Docker container (Dockerfile, docker-compose.yml)
- **Planned**: Cloud hosting (AWS/GCP/Azure TBD)

**Environments (dev/staging/prod):**
- **Current**: Local development
