# Content Governance Standard Operating Procedure

**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** October 2024  
**Scope:** Mind Coach and all faith-integrated content  

## Overview

This SOP ensures clinical and theological quality across all Mind Coach content through structured review processes, clear accountability, and quality assurance measures.

## Review Boards

### Clinical Review Board
**Purpose**: Ensure all therapeutic content meets clinical standards and evidence-based practices.

**Composition**:
- Licensed Clinical Social Worker (LCSW)
- Licensed Marriage and Family Therapist (LMFT) 
- Licensed Professional Counselor (LPC)
- PhD in Clinical Psychology
- Crisis intervention specialist

**Scope**:
- All mental wellness exercises and instructions
- Crisis intervention protocols and emergency resources
- Therapeutic language and terminology
- Evidence-based practice compliance
- Safety protocols and disclaimers

**Review Criteria**:
- [ ] Clinical accuracy and evidence-based foundation
- [ ] Appropriate therapeutic boundaries
- [ ] Crisis safety and emergency protocols
- [ ] Accessibility and inclusivity
- [ ] Professional disclaimers and limitations

### Theology Review Board
**Purpose**: Ensure doctrinal accuracy and respectful faith integration.

**Composition**:
- Ordained minister (multiple denominations)
- Theology PhD
- Biblical studies expert
- Denominational representative
- Faith integration specialist

**Scope**:
- KJV scripture excerpts and references
- Faith-based content and overlays
- Prayer content and spiritual exercises
- Doctrinal accuracy and theological consistency
- Interdenominational respect and inclusion

**Review Criteria**:
- [ ] Doctrinal accuracy and biblical fidelity
- [ ] KJV compliance (≤2 sentences per excerpt)
- [ ] Respectful tone and inclusive language
- [ ] Appropriate faith integration
- [ ] Interdenominational sensitivity

## Risk Assessment Matrix

| Risk Level | Content Type | Review Required | SLA |
|------------|--------------|-----------------|-----|
| **Low** | UI copy, navigation text, general instructions | Self-review | 7 days |
| **Medium** | Exercise instructions, wellness content, general faith content | Single board review | 5 days |
| **High** | Therapeutic exercises, scripture content, crisis resources | Dual board review | 3 days |
| **Critical** | Crisis protocols, emergency resources, core therapeutic content | Full board review + external validation | 48 hours |

*SLAs apply when an external advisor is engaged.*

## Review Process

### 1. Content Creation
- Content creator identifies risk level
- Assigns appropriate review board(s)
- Submits with required documentation

### 2. Board Review
- Board members review against criteria
- Provide feedback and recommendations
- Approve, request revisions, or reject

### 3. Sign-off and Implementation
- Final approval from designated board lead
- Content implementation with version control
- Post-launch monitoring and feedback collection

### 4. Quality Assurance
- Automated validation checks
- User feedback monitoring
- Regular content audits

## Quality Assurance Measures

### Automated Validation
- **KJV Verse Length**: Automated check for ≤2 sentences
- **Clinical Terminology**: Consistency validation
- **Crisis Language**: Detection and flagging system
- **Accessibility**: WCAG compliance verification
- **Content Versioning**: Change tracking and rollback capability

### Manual Audits
- **Monthly**: Random content sampling
- **Quarterly**: Full content review
- **Annually**: Complete content audit and update

### User Feedback Integration
- **Crisis Reports**: Immediate escalation protocol
- **Content Concerns**: 24-hour response SLA
- **Improvement Suggestions**: Monthly review and implementation

## Emergency Protocols

### Crisis Content Issues
1. **Immediate**: Remove content from production
2. **Within 1 hour**: Notify clinical board lead
3. **Within 4 hours**: Full board review and decision
4. **Within 24 hours**: Corrected content or permanent removal

### Doctrinal Concerns
1. **Immediate**: Flag content for review
2. **Within 4 hours**: Theology board assessment
3. **Within 24 hours**: Resolution or content removal
4. **Within 48 hours**: Communication to affected users
