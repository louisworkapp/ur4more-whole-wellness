# Discipleship: The 12 Core Foundations (12 Weeks) — KJV

**XP:** 150 per week | **Time:** ~45 min/week | **Gate:** Disciple/KingdomBuilder faith mode

Twelve essentials for following Jesus—from grace and new birth to obedience, community, mission, and perseverance.

---

## Week 1 — The Gospel of Grace (Justification by Faith)
- **KJV**: Eph 2:8–9; Rom 3:24–26; 2 Cor 5:21
- **Unlocking Truth (KJV)**: *Rom 3:24* — "Being justified freely by his grace through the redemption that is in Christ Jesus:"
- **Summary**: Saved by grace through faith; Christ our righteousness.
- **Practice**: 
  - Daily 5-min thanksgiving for grace.
  - Memorize: Ephesians 2:8–9 (KJV).
- **Reflect**: 
  - Where do I still try to earn God's favor?
  - How does justification change how I see myself today?

---

## Week 2 — Regeneration — New Birth by the Spirit
- **KJV**: Jn 3:3–8; Tit 3:5; Ezek 36:26–27; 2 Cor 5:17
- **Unlocking Truth (KJV)**: *Tit 3:5* — "Not by works of righteousness which we have done, but according to his mercy he saved us, by the washing of regeneration, and renewing of the Holy Ghost;"
- **Summary**: Not just forgiven—made new by the Spirit.
- **Practice**: 
  - Lectio on John 3:1–8; journal one sentence of response.
  - Memorize: Titus 3:5 (KJV).
- **Reflect**: 
  - Where do I see signs of new birth?
  - What old patterns are losing power?

---

## Week 3 — Adam → Christ & Union with Christ
- **KJV**: Rom 5:12–19; 1 Cor 15:22; 1 Cor 15:45–49; Rom 6:3–5
- **Unlocking Truth (KJV)**: *1 Cor 15:22* — "For as in Adam all die, even so in Christ shall all be made alive."
- **Summary**: In Adam all die; in Christ we live—united with Him.
- **Practice**: 
  - Read Romans 6:3–5; write how union with Christ changes your week.
  - Memorize: 1 Corinthians 15:22 (KJV).
- **Reflect**: 
  - Where do I still live 'in Adam'?
  - How does union with Christ empower obedience?

---

## Week 4 — Repentance & Faith — The Ongoing Posture
- **KJV**: Mk 1:15; Acts 3:19; 1 Jn 1:9
- **Unlocking Truth (KJV)**: *Mk 1:15* — "…repent ye, and believe the gospel."
- **Summary**: Turning from sin to God daily, by grace.
- **Practice**: 
  - Daily examen: confess & receive cleansing.
  - Memorize: Mark 1:15 (KJV).
- **Reflect**: 
  - What is God inviting me to turn from?
  - How does faith reshape my choices?

---

## Week 5 — Lordship & Obedience (Faith Working in Love)
- **KJV**: Jn 14:15; Jas 2:17; Gal 5:6; Mt 7:24–27
- **Unlocking Truth (KJV)**: *Jas 2:17* — "Even so faith, if it hath not works, is dead, being alone."
- **Summary**: Grace → obedience; love acts.
- **Practice**: 
  - Pick one command of Jesus and practice it this week.
  - Memorize: John 14:15 (KJV).
- **Reflect**: 
  - Where is love asking me to act?
  - What hinders my obedience?

---

## Week 6 — Scripture — Authority, Meditation, Obedience
- **KJV**: 2 Tim 3:16–17; Josh 1:8; Ps 1:2–3; Heb 4:12
- **Unlocking Truth (KJV)**: *Josh 1:8* — "…thou shalt meditate therein day and night…"
- **Summary**: Rooted in the Word; fruitful in season.
- **Practice**: 
  - 5–10 min daily reading + 2 min meditation.
  - Memorize: 2 Timothy 3:16 (KJV).
- **Reflect**: 
  - When do I resist the Word?
  - What habit will keep Scripture central?

---

## Week 7 — Prayer (and Fasting) — Aligning the Heart
- **KJV**: Mt 6:9–13; Phil 4:6–7; 1 Thess 5:17
- **Unlocking Truth (KJV)**: *Phil 4:6* — "Be careful for nothing…"
- **Summary**: Relational prayer that shapes desire.
- **Practice**: 
  - Set 3 one-minute prayer alarms.
  - Memorize: Matthew 6:9–13 (KJV).
- **Reflect**: 
  - What changes when I pray first?
  - Where is God inviting deeper trust?

---

## Week 8 — The Holy Spirit — Walk, Fruit, Power
- **KJV**: Rom 8:5–14; Gal 5:16–25; Acts 1:8
- **Unlocking Truth (KJV)**: *Gal 5:16* — "This I say then, Walk in the Spirit, and ye shall not fulfil the lust of the flesh."
- **Summary**: Indwelling presence for transformation and witness.
- **Practice**: 
  - Morning surrender prayer to the Spirit.
  - Memorize: Galatians 5:16 (KJV).
- **Reflect**: 
  - Where do I see fruit forming?
  - What quenches the Spirit in me?

---

## Week 9 — Identity & Holiness
- **KJV**: 1 Pet 2:9; Rom 12:1–2; 1 Thess 4:3
- **Unlocking Truth (KJV)**: *1 Pet 2:9* — "…a chosen generation…"
- **Summary**: Who we are → how we live.
- **Practice**: 
  - Replace one lie with truth each day.
  - Memorize: Romans 12:2 (KJV).
- **Reflect**: 
  - Which identity statement anchors me?
  - Where do I need renewal?

---

## Week 10 — Community, Confession, and the Church
- **KJV**: Heb 10:24–25; Acts 2:42; Jas 5:16
- **Unlocking Truth (KJV)**: *Heb 10:24* — "…provoke unto love and to good works:"
- **Summary**: We grow together; confession heals.
- **Practice**: 
  - Attend a group/1:1 this week.
  - Memorize: James 5:16 (KJV).
- **Reflect**: 
  - Where do I hide? Why?
  - How can I encourage someone this week?

---

## Week 11 — Mission — Witness & Service
- **KJV**: Mt 28:18–20; 1 Pet 3:15; Mk 10:45; Mt 5:16
- **Unlocking Truth (KJV)**: *1 Pet 3:15* — "…be ready always to give an answer…"
- **Summary**: Share hope and serve like Jesus.
- **Practice**: 
  - Share a faith conversation or invite.
  - Memorize: Matthew 5:16 (KJV).
- **Reflect**: 
  - What part of my story shows grace?
  - Who am I praying for?

---

## Week 12 — Perseverance & Rule of Life (Abide & Continue)
- **KJV**: Jn 15:5; Jas 1:2–4; Col 2:6–7; Ps 90:12
- **Unlocking Truth (KJV)**: *Jn 15:5* — "…for without me ye can do nothing."
- **Summary**: Stable rhythms for the long journey.
- **Practice**: 
  - Write & share your rule of life.
  - Memorize: Colossians 2:6–7 (KJV).
- **Reflect**: 
  - Which rhythms help me abide?
  - Who will walk with me from here?
